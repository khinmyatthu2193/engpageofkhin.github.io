26.Talking about jobs and occupations


							
				What do you do?
				What do you do for a living?
				I’ve got a part-time job.
				Where do you work?
				I work from home.
				I work in a factory.
				What is your occupation?
				I work as a doctor.
				I’m looking for a job.
				I’m a teacher.
				I’m not working at the moment.
				I’m unemployed.
				I do the paperwork.
				I have my own business.
				Do like your job?
